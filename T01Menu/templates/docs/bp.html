1. Executive Summary

Skip The Line is an innovative platform designed to enhance the food and beverage experience at live events such as fairs, concerts, and sports games. By allowing attendees to order food and drinks directly from their seats, our platform eliminates the need for waiting in long lines, providing unparalleled convenience and boosting sales for vendors. The service targets high-footfall venues, focusing on areas with at least 1,000 persons per hour, ensuring a consistent and substantial user base.

3. Market Research

Target Market:

  • Primary: Event venues such as stadiums, fairgrounds, and concert halls with significant foot traffic.
  • Secondary: Food vendors, restaurants, and catering companies operating within these venues.

Market Needs:

  • Attendees want to enjoy events without the disruption of standing in lines.
  • Vendors need efficient order management systems to handle large volumes and increase sales.

Market Trends:

  • Growing integration of technology in event management.
  • Increasing demand for convenience and time-saving solutions in crowded environments.
  • High adoption rates of mobile payment systems.

Competitive Analysis:

  • Direct Competitors: Existing venue-specific ordering apps and in-seat service providers.
  • Indirect Competitors: Traditional concession stands and mobile food vendors.
  • Competitive Advantage: Unlike many competitors, Convince Matters offers a user-friendly platform that integrates seamlessly with existing vendor systems and provides real-time delivery tracking.

4. Marketing and Sales Strategy

Marketing Objectives:

  • Establish Skip The Line as the preferred in-seat service provider at large venues.
  • Drive user adoption through targeted digital marketing and strategic partnerships.

Marketing Strategies:

  • Digital Marketing: Utilize social media, targeted ads, and influencer partnerships to reach event-goers.
  • Partnerships: Collaborate with major event venues and food vendors to integrate our service and offer exclusive promotions.
  • Referral Programs: Encourage attendees to refer friends and family with discounts on future orders.

Sales Strategy:

  • Vendor Outreach: Directly engage with vendors to onboard them onto the platform, emphasizing the benefits of increased sales and operational efficiency.
  • Event Organizer Collaboration: Work with event organizers to integrate Skip The Line into their event management, providing them with a value-added service for attendees.
  • Customer Retention: Implement loyalty programs to encourage repeat use of the platform by event-goers.
